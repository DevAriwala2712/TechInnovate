📜 Milestones in Technology

Explore key breakthroughs that shaped our world.

🌕 1969 - Apollo 11 Moon Landing

NASA's Apollo 11 mission put the first humans on the moon, marking a historic step in space exploration.

🌐 1983 - The Birth of the Internet

ARPANET transitioned to the TCP/IP protocol, creating the foundation for the modern internet.

♟️ 1997 - AI Beats Chess Champion

IBM's Deep Blue defeated world chess champion Garry Kasparov, proving AI could challenge human intelligence.

📱 2007 - The iPhone Revolution

Apple launched the iPhone, transforming the smartphone industry with a touchscreen interface.

🧠 2015 - AI & Deep Learning Boom

Breakthroughs in AI, including Google's AlphaGo, showed how deep learning could surpass human capabilities.

🤖 2023 - ChatGPT Takes Over

Generative AI became mainstream with tools like ChatGPT and Midjourney, revolutionizing content creation.
